About

what we do and why

desk is a Canadian-born company and a producer of high quality stationary products, such as pens and notebooks. We're a diverse community of individuals with the goal to create a flexible, adaptative products for all your creative and business needs. We chose the name "desk" for it's simplicity and because you can count on us to be a stable provider for stationary products

brand history

desk was started in 2005 in Vancouver, Canada by the Richards family. Abigail Richards, the Co-Founder, was a teacher and was disatisfied with the stationary products supplied to her school as well as other options on the market. She found them to be either to expensive or lacking in quality and decided to pursue other options. That lead to eventual brainstorming with her husband Frank Richards, an Engineer, and desk was born. They began work on crafting higher quality products at a rate that teachers and others in her community could afford. Since then, desk has grown to over 5,000 employees and locations all around Canada.

brand philosophies

When desk was started, the goal was to create high-quality products at a cheaper price and in our 15 years in business, we have not wavered from that philosophy. We here at desk don't believe price and quality should be mutually exclusive and want our customer to feel like they can still rely on our products despite the low price tag. Our aim create a stable relationship between our product and customer so you can always count on us to meet your stationary needs.

brand mission

Our mission here at desk, is to create a relationship with our customers that you can trust and rely on.

And keep your desk clean.
Because a clear desk, is a clear mind.
